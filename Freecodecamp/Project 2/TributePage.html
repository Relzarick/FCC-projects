Nier Automata

Nier: Automata is a 2017 action role-playing game developed by PlatinumGames

Production began in 2014, with series creator Yoko Taro, producer Yosuke Saito, and lead composer Keiichi Okabe reprising their roles in Nier.
Nier: Automata was announced at E3 2015
Nier: Automata was released for Windows personal computers (PCs) through Steam on March 17, 2017.
Sales surpassed expectations and as of April 2023, the game had shipped 7.5 million copies worldwide
